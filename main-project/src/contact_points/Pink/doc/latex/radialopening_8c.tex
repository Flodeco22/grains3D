radial opening by line 

{\bfseries{Usage\+:}} radialopening in.\+pgm length [angle in degrees] [dilatmask.\+pgm] out.\+pgm

{\bfseries{Description\+:}} Radial opening by line (or dilated line). Max between the openings of in.\+pgm by a linear structuring element rotated by angle until 360 degrees and dilated by dilatmask.\+pgm. The angle is normalized to an exact division of 360 by an integer.
